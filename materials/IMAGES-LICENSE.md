## Purpose

These images are the stimuli shown to survey participants during the
experimental conditions described in the article. They are released into
the public domain so that other researchers may freely reuse them for
replication studies, meta-analyses, or new experimental designs.

## Contents

Images depict hypothetical use scenarios involving photographs, advertisements,
and other creative works. See the article and preregistration for full
descriptions of each experimental vignette.
