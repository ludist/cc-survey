# Thomas H. Rousse

**J.D., Ph.D.** · Northwestern University

I study open licensing, innovation policy, and the law of creative industries. My research uses experimental and computational methods to examine how legal frameworks shape creative production and public understanding of intellectual property.

## Current Research

- **Creative Commons and copyright risk perception** — A national survey experiment examining whether CC licenses help people understand what they can do with creative works. ([repo](https://github.com/ludist/cc-survey) · [project site](https://ludist.github.io/cc-survey/))
- **Indie game innovation** — How independent game developers navigate IP regimes
- **Fair use perception** — Public understanding of fair use, transformative works, and chimerical cloning
